# AI Use Policy

AI-assisted tooling is permitted. Contributors are responsible for reviewing and validating all changes before submitting them. By contributing, you affirm that you understand and accept responsibility for the correctness, safety, and licensing implications of your changes, regardless of whether AI tools were used.

## Expectations
- If you use an AI agent on this repository, it must follow the instructions in [AGENTS.md](AGENTS.md).
- Review all AI-generated code and documentation for correctness and security.
- Ensure changes comply with project guidelines and licensing.
- Do not submit AI-generated content that includes proprietary, confidential, or third-party source material without permission.

## Maintainer Rights
Maintainers may request additional context or testing evidence for AI-assisted changes and may reject changes that do not meet project standards.
